About me

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Application Technology, M.S. degree in Computer System Architecture, and B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Jilin University.

 

Research Interests

My research areas are distributed computing and high-performance computing, focusing on resource scheduling and fault tolerant for networked systems.
